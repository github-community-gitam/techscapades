Hey Geeks !!

Are you ready for a quest by putting your techincal knowledge to the test?

Techscapades is the first-ever Technical Treasure Hunt hosted by GitHub Community GITAM.

Eash team would have two hours to follow the clues around campus to find the hidden surprise.

Each team must consist of four members.

Event date: 20th December 2022

  • Objective of the teams is to solve clues to collect points and unlock the clue to the next step of the hunt. This sets the team through a journey of clues and locations
  • Progression through the hunt will be monitored and must be through questions assigned to individual teams.
  • If a random QR code is scanned which is not related to the path the team is taking, teams will be prompted with an error message and must solve the questions properly to finish the hunt.
  • It is mandatory that each team collects all the clues intended to qualify for a win. Teams finding the treasure without collecting all the clues will be disqualified.
  • Teams must reach the required number of points before reaching the finish line. Any inconsistencies with the score will result in disqualification.
  • First team to reach the treasure and scan the final QR code wins the treasure hunt.
  • Captain of the team has to login using the credentials set up during registration. Teams that have signed up using the google forms must use the captain’s GITAM email as username and captain’s contact number as password.
  • On the dashboard, the top left hand corner displays the team's name and current score.
  • In the middle, a toggle camera button and logout button can be found. Toggle camera button can be used to turn the camera on or off when necessary.
  • All questions will be displayed upon scanning a QR code hidden in all the locations corresponding to the hunt. After scanning the hidden QR code, answer the question to find the next step of the treasure hunt.
  • If accidentally logged out or kicked out due to a network issue, simply log back in and scan the QR code once again to receive the question.
  • After solving the last question of the hunt, teams will be prompted on the completion of the hunt.
